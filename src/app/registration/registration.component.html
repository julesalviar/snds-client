TAX INCENTIVES FOR DEPED PARTNERS

The Department of Education (DepEd) recognizes the important role of the Private Sector in the promotion of quality and accessible education. As a way of recognizing the active involvement of the Private Sector in the implementation of the K to 12 Program, the DepEd supports the tax incentivization campaign of the Bureau of Internal Revenue.

Through the implementation of the tax incentive provision of Philippine Republic Act No. 8525, “Adopt-a-School Act of 1998”, private entities are given the opportunity to help public schools, and these adopting entities become eligible for tax incentive claims.

The Department of Education invites all members of the private sector to accept the offer of partnerships to help boost education opportunities for the young.

Who are qualified

  • Companies
  • Business Enterprises
  • Foundations
  • Civil Society Organizations
  • International Organizations and Associations
  • Private Schools /Universities /Colleges
  • Private Individuals (local and abroad)
