What is School Needs Data System (SNDS)?

➢ It is a web-based partnership system that serves as an information hub reflecting all the needs of the schools/learning centers based on their respective Annual Improvement Plans (AIP).

School Needs Data Goal

  • Strengthen public-private partnerships to generate continuous support that is responsive to the needs of the learners.

What does SNDS offer?

  • Provide stakeholders with a clear picture of the educational needs of the schools, districts, and divisions (school needs menu is categorized into infra and infra and a special feature that profiles our senior high school learners).
  • Makes partnership engagement easier.
  • Facilitates purposive engagement.
  • Facilitates equitable distribution of resources.
  • Track utilization of funds coming from School MOOE, School Canteen, School IGP, SPTA, BLGU, LGU, SEF, National Funded Programs & Projects, and other sources to support the accomplishment of the AIP.
  • Monitor partnership initiatives entered into by public schools/LCs and their stakeholders.
  • Serves as a feedback mechanism between the school and stakeholders – (i.e., the status of the program & progress report).
  • Provides portal for our skilled senior high school graduates who are looking for work.
  • Provides portal for our senior high school learners who are looking for work immersion venue.
  • Consolidate stakeholders’ profiles.
  • Consolidate generated resources at the school, district, & division level through partnership.
  • Ensures that stakeholders are working along with the plans indicated in the AIP.
  • Provides relevant information for our program holders that aids in conducting Quality Assurance, Technical Assistance, & Monitoring & Evaluation.
  • Serves as a relevant tool for every program holder in tracking the implementation of their respective program in the school.
