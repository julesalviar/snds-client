What is School Needs Data System (SNDS)?

➢ It is a web-based partnership system that serves as an information hub reflecting all the needs of the schools/learning centers based on their respective Annual Improvement Plans (AIP).

School Needs Data Goal

  • Strengthen public-private partnerships to generate continuous support that is responsive to the needs of the learners.

What does SNDS offer?

    ➢ School Level

  • serves as a relevant tool for resource and project management
  • tracks utilization of funds coming from School MOOE, School Canteen, School IGP, SPTA, BLGU, LGU, SEF, National Funded Programs & Projects, and other sources to support the accomplishment of the AIP.
  • serves as a feedback mechanism between the school and stakeholders – (i.e., the status of the program & transparency)
  • ensures that stakeholders are working along with the plans indicated in the AIP
  • consolidates generated resources and profiles stakeholders
  • generates DPDS report

    ➢ Division Level

  • provide stakeholders with a clear picture of the educational needs of the schools, districts, and divisions (school needs menu is categorized into infra and non-infra and a special feature that profiles our senior high school learners)
  • facilitates equitable distribution of resources
  • monitor partnership initiatives entered by public schools/LCs and their stakeholders
  • consolidate generated resources at the school, district, & division level through partnership
  • consolidates stakeholders’ profile
  • facilitates ease of monitoring and evaluation of program holders' implementation of their annual plans
  • provides relevant information for our program holders that aids in providing technical assistance and monitoring and evaluation.

    ➢ Stakeholder Level

  • provides access to relevant information for future engagements
  • makes partnership engagement easier
  • facilitates purposive engagement
  • serves as a feedback mechanism between the school and stakeholders – (i.e., the status of the program & transparency)
